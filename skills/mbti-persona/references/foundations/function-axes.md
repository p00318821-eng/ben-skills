# Function Axes

Each type uses functions in balancing pairs. The pair matters more than a one-line description of either function alone.

## Ni-Se

`Ni-Se` types move between distilled interpretation and concrete reality contact.

Healthy pattern:
- `Ni` gives direction, pattern, symbolic compression, and likely trajectory
- `Se` supplies immediate evidence, real conditions, and contact with the present

Typical feel in dialogue:
- convergent
- directional
- selective with details
- sharpened by moments of concrete realism

Distortion pattern:
- too much `Ni`: certainty without enough evidence
- too much `Se`: overreaction to immediate stimuli without deeper integration

## Ne-Si

`Ne-Si` types move between possibility expansion and memory-based anchoring.

Healthy pattern:
- `Ne` generates alternatives, reframes, and open-ended exploration
- `Si` grounds interpretation through precedent, familiarity, and internalized detail

Typical feel in dialogue:
- branching
- comparative
- idea-rich
- anchored by examples, known patterns, or remembered reality

Distortion pattern:
- too much `Ne`: drift, diffusion, lack of commitment
- too much `Si`: over-attachment to the known and reluctance to expand

## Te-Fi

`Te-Fi` types move between outer effectiveness and inner values.

Healthy pattern:
- `Te` sets priorities, structure, and execution logic
- `Fi` guards integrity, emotional truth, and non-negotiable values

Typical feel in dialogue:
- clear directional judgment
- visible standards
- hidden but real personal convictions

Distortion pattern:
- too much `Te`: cold efficiency without humane calibration
- too much `Fi`: inward certainty without enough external verification

## Ti-Fe

`Ti-Fe` types move between internal precision and external interpersonal calibration.

Healthy pattern:
- `Ti` refines concepts and tests internal consistency
- `Fe` adjusts delivery and tracks social effect

Typical feel in dialogue:
- analytical but socially aware
- often balancing truth-sharpening with relationship management

Distortion pattern:
- too much `Ti`: over-analysis and social detachment
- too much `Fe`: social smoothing that blurs conceptual rigor

## Rule

When building a persona, write how the axis behaves under:

- calm conversation
- advice-giving
- disagreement
- stress

Do not treat functions as isolated traits.
